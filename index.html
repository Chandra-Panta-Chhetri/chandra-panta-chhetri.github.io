Welcome to Team Work

A skillful group of CSCC01 students building an MVP that will replace togotoronto.com and showcase local restaurants and restaurant owners in Scarborough.

The Problem
  • Scarborough food can only say so much alone. Chefs and owners have story to tell, but who is listening?
  • Downtown and uptown Toronto dining get all the attention, including the togotoronto.com website
  • Scarborough food is often lower margin and paying delivery services 30% is not sustainable, especially during the pandemic
Goals
  • Create an outstanding platform where restaurant owners and chefs of Scarborough can tell their story and inspire people to try their food
  • Promote local restaurants in Scarborough and improve the engagement and number of patrons accessing local food.
  • Hone our development skills while successfully follow current best software engineering practices
Strengths
  • Team members have a personal connection to each other and this will greatly impact how well our team does
  • As our team name suggests, we have excellent teamwork skills
